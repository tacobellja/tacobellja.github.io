Cocktail B52 Recetas e Ingredientes (Origen) ¿Cómo hacer?

The B-52 drink (or B52) is a layered cocktail made with a coffee liqueur, an Irish cream liqueur and an orange flavored liqueur . The ingredients are layered one top of the other such that three distinct colored layers are visible. This drink is usually made as a shooter in a shot glass. And it is expected to be consumed as a shooter…in one swig.
