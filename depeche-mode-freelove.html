Freelove — Depeche Mode Discography

" Freelove " is a song by English electronic group Depeche Mode. It was released on 5 November 2001 as the third single from their album, Exciter (2001). "Freelove" reached No. 19 on the UK Singles Chart. The single version, which was produced by Flood, is different than the album version, with a shorter running time and an additional drum track.
