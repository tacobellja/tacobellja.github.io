Ligabue: biography. Antonio Ligabue was born on December 18, 1899, in Zurich, Switzerland: his mother was Elisabetta Costa, an emigrant from the village of Cencenighe Agordino, near Belluno.It is not known who the father is, and at the registry office in the Swiss city the infant is registered as Antonio Costa.In September 1900 he was placed in the care of a local couple, Johannes Valentin.
