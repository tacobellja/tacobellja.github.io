"Bella senz'anima" is an Italian ballad song written by Marco Luberti, Paolo Casella and Riccardo Cocciante, arranged by Franco Pisano and performed by Riccardo Cocciante. It was the first single from the Cocciante's 1974 album Anima. The song was launched by Cocciante during a tour ("Racconto") he made in 1973 together with Antonello Venditti.
